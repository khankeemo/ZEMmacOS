PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 2026

Websmith Digital ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy
Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
   a) Device Information: Hardware identifiers (CPU ID, motherboard serial,
      MAC address), operating system version, device name
   b) License Information: License keys, activation status, trial status
   c) Usage Data: Runtime duration, feature usage, error reports
   d) Contact Information: Email address (for trial OTP, license communications)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
   a) License validation and enforcement
   b) Hardware binding verification
   c) Trial management
   d) Product improvement and analytics
   e) Customer support
   f) Security monitoring and fraud prevention

3. DATA STORAGE
   a) License data is stored on secure servers
   b) Local cache is encrypted using AES-256-GCM
   c) Data is retained as long as your license is active
   d) Trial data is retained for 30 days after trial expiry

4. DATA SHARING
   We do NOT sell your personal information. Data may be shared with:
   a) Payment processors (for paid transactions)
   b) Email service providers (for transactional communications)
   c) Legal authorities (when required by law)

5. SECURITY
   a) All API communications use HMAC-SHA256 signed requests
   b) Local data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM
   c) Server communications use TLS encryption
   d) Rate limiting protects against abuse

6. YOUR RIGHTS
   You have the right to:
   a) Access your personal data
   b) Request data deletion
   c) Withdraw consent (may affect license functionality)
   d) Contact us regarding privacy concerns

7. CONTACT
   Privacy inquiries: support@websmithdigital.com
   Address: Websmith Digital, Kolkata, West Bengal, India

By clicking "Agree" you consent to the collection and use of your
information as described in this Privacy Policy.
